Chip Butty

Recipe description

An extremely simple British dish, commonly consumed after a night out and provides a great way to sort your stomach out, after a few too much. Cheap and easy to make, you can't go wrong with this dish.

ingredients

  • Chips: they can be oven chips or homecooked chips, it's up to you.
  • Bread: the foundation of the dish.
  • Butter: to coat the inside of the sandwich.

Steps

  1. Cook the chips.
  2. Whilst the chips are cooking, butter the bread as you would a sandwich.
  3. Fill the sandwich with the cooked chips.
  4. Optional: add tomato ketchup, or any sauce of the choice inside the sandwich.
