Lasagna

Recipe description

Lasagna is an extremely popular dish across the globe, and for good reason too! Just by simply layering a few ingredients, you can really wow your family or dinner guests. This recipe is incredibly customisable too, just add or replace certain ingredients, for a different flavour.

ingredients

  • Meat: lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: cooked with meat to add flavour.
  • Tomato product: lots of tomato sauce, crushed tomato and tomato paste.
  • Sugar: two tablespoons of white sugar to add a subtle sweetness to the dish.
  • Spice and seasonings: fresh parsley, salt, basil, and black pepper.
  • Pasta sheets: a pack of lasagna sheets to layer.
  • Cheese: to layer on top of the dish.
