Cottage Pie

Recipe description

A classic British dish, perfect for the colder months, or in the cases of Britain, every day! Meaty and fulfilling you can't go wrong with this family dish!

ingredients

  • Meat: lean ground beef.
  • Onion: adds flavour to the dish when cooked with meat.
  • Herbs and seasonings: italian seasoning, ground cinnamon, fresh parsley, salt, and pepper.
  • Vegetables: carrots and peas go well.
  • Stock: one beef stock cube.
  • Tomato products: tomato paste and chopped tomato.
  • Potatoes: 4 peeled, diced and boiled potatoes.
  • Milk and butter: for your mash.
  • Cheese: to top the dish
